We propose 5 steps to your
total three-leveled safety with our
PYTHON system

1
Your safety starts with containment double-walled pipes. Working pipe inside the external tube is the basic and universally recognized safety idea. You can choose pipes from any manufacturer and we will work with them. However, if you want to go the best way, let’s follow our own technology
Learn more
2
Depending on the liquid or gas you use, our chemists will select an effective neutralizing solution. Our technology assumes that you will need an extremely small neutralizing solution volume but it needed for your best safety!
Learn more
3
Using compressed air, we create a vacuum in the space between the double walls of the pipelines. The automatic safety system will see if the pressure inside grows too fast. This is the safety level No. 1!
Learn more
4
There are no pipelines without armature. Unfortunately, the installation places are most susceptible to leaks. We use our special suction technology, which creates the best safety for any valve cabinets. Even with the opened doors! This is the safety level No. 2!
Learn more
5
Both parts of the system - vacuum and suction are able to remove dangerous fractions into neutralization module filled with special solution before the automatics block the broken pipeline and open another one. This is the safety level No. 3!
Learn more
